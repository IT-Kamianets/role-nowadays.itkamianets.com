# Mafia Mode

Mafia Mode is a social deduction game where players receive secret roles and must use discussion, deception, and deduction to win. The game alternates between **night phases** (role actions) and **day phases** (discussion). Each role has a specific ability that influences the outcome of the game.

The goal depends on the role:  
- **Village team** tries to eliminate all hostile roles.  
- **Hostile roles** try to eliminate the village or reach parity.

---

# Players

Minimum players:

**5**

Recommended players:

**7–10**

Maximum players:

**15**

With fewer than 5 players the deduction aspect becomes too weak, and with more than 15 the game becomes slow and difficult to manage.

---

# Game Flow

Each round has two phases.

## Night

Roles perform their actions secretly.

Examples:
- Mafia choose a target
- Doctor protects someone
- Detective investigates someone

Actions are resolved by the server.

---

## Day

Players discuss what happened during the night.

Players may accuse, defend, or bluff.

At the end of the day a **vote** may occur depending on the mode settings.

The player with the most votes is eliminated.

---

# Victory Conditions

Village wins when:

- All mafia or hostile roles are eliminated.

Mafia wins when:

- The number of mafia is equal to or greater than the number of villagers.

Neutral roles may have their own special win conditions.

---

# Roles

Below is a list of roles that can be used to create different game modes.

---

# Village Roles

## Villager
No special abilities.

## Detective
Each night investigates a player and learns if they belong to the mafia team.

## Doctor
Protects one player each night from being eliminated.

## Bodyguard
Protects a player and may die instead of the protected target.

## Sheriff
Can publicly reveal investigation results.

## Tracker
Learns which player their target visited during the night.

## Watcher
Learns who visited a specific player.

## Priest
Can remove negative effects or revive once depending on mode.

## Mayor
Their vote counts as two votes.

---

# Mafia Roles

## Mafia
Standard mafia member that participates in the nightly kill.

## Godfather
Appears innocent to investigation roles.

## Consigliere
Investigates roles instead of alignment.

## Roleblocker
Prevents a target from using their ability.

## Poisoner
Applies poison that kills after several rounds.

## Framer
Makes a player appear guilty to investigation roles.

---

# Neutral Roles

## Jester
Wins if they are voted out by players.

## Executioner
Has a secret target and wins if that player is eliminated.

## Survivor
Wins if alive at the end of the game.

## Serial Killer
Kills a player each night and wins by being the last alive.

## Arsonist
Marks players and can eliminate all marked players at once.

---

# Custom Modes

Game creators can combine roles to create custom setups such as:

- Classic Mafia
- Extended Roles
- Chaos Mode
- Minimal Mode
- Hidden Roles Mode
